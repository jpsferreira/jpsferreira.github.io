About Me

I'm a 31-year-old Mechanical Engineer from Porto (Portugal) currently working as Head of Artificial Intelligence and Data Science at DigestAID. I am also an Assistant Professor and Researcher at University of Porto doing supervision, mentoring, and managing research teams as a Principal Investigator.

My research path has been marked by measuring forces in biological systems at the micro and macro scales. I am a mechanical engineer able to find fast answers for medical services using engineering approaches. I have worked in diverse fields and I have applied several practical and research skills that gave me a unique and integrative perspectives to deal with technical problems. I have 50+ contributions in scientific papers, secured multiple competitive research projects, and filled six patents.

I am a curious professional with good leadership and communication skills. I have great motivation to apply my experience and abilities in the design and implementation of projects that guarantee the deliverables and correct scientific development. More than seven years of research in engineering have given me significant knowledge of research management and critical analytical and innovation skills. I plan strategically and implement engineered approaches to reach goals and results faster.
