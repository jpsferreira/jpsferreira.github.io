Material Characterisation

Explored the mechanical properties and stretching limits of biological tissues under various loading conditions. Covered experimental methods for characterizing different tissue types, advanced curve fitting, data cleaning, and statistical analyses. Accessed a curated list of related scientific papers and generated data for model development.

Atomic Force Microscopy

Investigated the mechanics of cells using Atomic Force Microscopy (AFM). Studied indentation on smooth muscle cells, applied time-series curve analysis, and applied robust statistical analysis. Learn why AFM is a powerful tool for cellular biomechanics and access the full MATLAB analysis code on GitHub.

Force Transmission in Living Matter

Applied continuum mechanics of force transmission within cells. Applied tensor theory and microstructural modeling for soft tissues and for cytoskeleton. Developed a general framework to implement programming of user material subroutines (UMAT) in ABAQUS user material subroutines (UMAT) in ABAQUS, a finite element analysis software. Shared a library of UMATs for multiple materials library of UMATs.

About Me
